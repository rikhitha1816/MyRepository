public cloud:
Accessible: Available to the general public over the internet.
Scalable: Resources can be easily scaled up or down based on demand.
Shared Infrastructure: Multiple users share the same physical hardware.
Resource Pooling: Computing resources are pooled together and dynamically allocated.
Self-Service: Users can provision and manage resources without provider intervention.
Pay-Per-Use: Users pay only for the resources they consume.
Managed Services: Providers offer various managed services like databases, security, etc.
